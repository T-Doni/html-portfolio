  • Mac&Cheese

    • Quick, easy, and tasty macaroni and cheese dish.

    Ingredients:

    • macaroni
    • 1/4 cup butter
    • 1/2 teaspoon salt
    • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
    • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
    • 2 cups of milk

    Instructions:

    1. Cook macaroni in boiling water 8 minutes.
    2. At the same time, melt butter on pan over medium heat
    3. Add salt and pepper, stir about 5 minutes
    4. Add milk while stirring, continue to stir until bubbling, make sure milk doesn't burn.
    5. Add cheddar and stir 2 to 4 minutes
    6. Drain macaroni and fold it with cheese sauce

  • Chicken Curry

    • Chicken curry is a globally popular dish featuring tender chicken simmered in a savory, spiced sauce

    Ingredients:

    • Rice
    • 1 lb chicken breast, cutted into bite-sized pieces
    • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
    • 2 tablespoons curry powder
    • 1 cup chicken broth
    • Salt and pepper to taste

    Instructions:

    1. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
    2. Add chicken pieces and cook until browned on all sides.
    3. Add curry powder and stir to coat the chicken.
    4. Pour in chicken broth, bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 20 minutes.
    5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
    6. Serve hot over cooked rice.

  • Kuurdak

    • Kuurdak is a traditional Kazakh dish made from fried meat and organs, often served with onions and flatbread.

    Ingredients:

    • 1 lb lamb or beef, cut into small pieces
    • 1 onion, sliced
    • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
    • Salt and pepper to taste

    Instructions:

    1. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
    2. Add meat pieces and cook until browned on all sides.
    3. Add sliced onions and continue to cook until onions are soft.
    4. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
    5. Serve hot with flatbread.
